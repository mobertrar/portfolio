Careers Course

This course is an interactive experience that trains people of all ages and from all walks of life to succeed in their vocation in live.

Communications Course

This course starts with storytelling, which is the basis of all good communication and an essential skill to master in today's media-rich world. The course continues with examples of communication in different media and concludes by training students in the arts of persuasion.

Fundraising Course

This course teaches the elements of fundraising and equips students to organize successful fundraising themselves.

A-Z of Event Planning Course

Events should be both innovative and creative in order to achieve a maximum reach, engage, excite, motivate and therefore succeed in political campaigns.
